codeArt

This is a collection of some interactive (JavaScript) projects I've made. Enjoy their playful, just-above-mundane nature.

isoBuilder

Jun. 2022. A minecraft-style isometric world builder. Click and place blocks of your choice. Use F to fill the current layer and Space to rotate the camera.

badListener

Jun. 2023. A CFG (context free grammar) sentence generator that really won't listen to your prompts. May accidentally impart wisdom.

tinyMen

Jun. 2023. You're just a cow in the world.

qp

May. 2025. A Two Player Game! Spam Q or P to win.

keyboardFight

May. 2025. Another Two Player Game... Fight across your keyboard to type words from your set (Desktop only).

asciiCam

May. 2023. Turns your webcam into ascii text. There's audio too - the brighter the image, the higher the frequency.

badListener2

Dec. 2023. Chatbot that listens a bit better, with an engaging interface. Based on ELIZA (1966).

tellMeAboutYourHobbies

May. 2025. A simpler, stripped back, web-based version of a part in Reflections on Identity---an interactive art installation I made for my dissertation.

zenGarden

May. 2023. Unwind with this wind-chime-like soundboard (C pentatonic). Squares get greener the more you play but some will darken again!

backButton

July. 2023. Go back to the way things were.

artTask

Jun. 2023. Generates a busywork task you can complete in 5 minutes to 45 minutes, along with artwork provided by the Art Institute of Chigaco API. Eccentric text to speech included :D

soCentered

July. 2023. Get centered. A silly little game where you try and click the center point between two squares.

tetrominoes

Jan. 2022. Fully complete tetris clone.
