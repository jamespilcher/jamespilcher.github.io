keyboardFight
Welcome to keyboardFight! Here's how to play:
  • Each player gets a set of letters from across the keyboard.
  • Form words by typing letters. Submit with Left Shift (Player 1) or Right Shift (Player 2).
  • Words must be valid and cannot be submitted twice.
  • Earn points based on the length of your word.
  • The game ends when the 60 second timer runs out---the player with the highest score wins!
Have fun :^)
