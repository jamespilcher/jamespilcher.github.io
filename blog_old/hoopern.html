This is something I’ve thought of doing before. Currently on the Shinkansen from Fukoyama to Tokyo, very tired, in the mood for something silly. I’m typing this on my phone.

I had this idea some time last year. In my head it featured Cody Mainwairing, my old media buddy, taking a tour of our then student house, 61 Hoopern Street. It's in a mock-documentary format, so largely filmed from Cody’s perspective. Anyway, consider this a first draft of act 1.

To Andrew Coombes

61 HOOPERN

(A Mockumentary)

by

James Pilcher

INTRO SEQUENCE.

Typical documentary sequence with a montage of clips and voice-over introducing the show.

CODY (VO)

The University of Exeter commissioned this documentary on behalf of first-year students looking into housing for next year. I’m looking forward to getting to know the residents here at 61 Hoopern. For those that don’t know, my name is Cody Mainwaring - but you can just call me Cody.

ACT ONE

EXT. 61 Hoopern front door - Morning.

Side shot of CODY knocking on the door. After the third knock, we see ALEX’s head (and upper torso, topless) leaning out of the front-facing window.

He makes a silly face at the camera - CODY doesn’t see. ALEX face becomes straight-faced, then

ALEX

You must be Charlie.

CODY looks over.

CODY

It's Cody.

ALEX’s head swung back inside (comically quickly) after “it’s”. He wasn’t listening.

On queue, the front door opens.

NAT

(Big grin, and like greeting an old friend)

Charlie!

NAT goes for a hug, raised on the doorway, CODY still on the street. Awkward height differential because of this. Cody reluctantly accepts.

CODY

(Muffled)

It’s Cody.

Hug completes.

NAT

You smell amazing. Come inside. Not like that obviously.

INT. 61 Hoopern entrance

As CODY steps in, ALEX’s bedroom door opens. ALEX is standing there, now clothed, but he has one sock on his foot, the other sock on his left hand like a glove. He goes for a handshake with the sockless hand.

ALEX

(Handshake commences)

Alex. I’m the sheriff ‘round these parts.

ALEX winks.

CODY

I’m Cody, nice to meet you

ALEX

(Gesturing to cameraman)

Have you two met?

CODY

Have me and the cameraman met? Yes we have.

ALEX

Ah okay, well I haven’t met them.

ALEX (CONT’D)

(Close to the camera, as if it’s the cameraman's eyes, in a baby voice)

Hello, Mr Cameraman. I’m Alex Gulliver.

NAT acts like this is normal behaviour, although he wouldn't do this himself.

CODY

I’d rather you didn’t do that.

Tension is cut by the sound of a few loud stomps, a tumble, and a few more stomps. A muffled ‘yooo’.

We then see and hear a loud tumble - BRET falling down the stairs. He gets up.

BRET

Yo guys - who smells amazing?

Short introduction montage. People shaking hands, even ALEX and BRET shaking hands.

CODY (VO)

It was clear there was something very strange going on in this house.

Returning from short introduction montage

CODY

I believe there are two more to meet.

BRET

Alright hungry boy.

Everyone visibly confused at BRET. Beat.

CODY

Excuse me?

BRET

(Caveman voice)

TWO MORE TWO MEAT.

NAT and ALEX react simultaneously.

NAT

That’s not what he meansss.

ALEX

Noo bret.

BRET looks sad and dejected. Reuben emerges from his room, acting normally. Goes for handshake

REUBEN

Hey, I’m Reuben, nice to meet you. I'm looking forward to this.

CODY

That’s great. I’m Cody, nice to meet you too.

NAT and ALEX look at each other and mouthing “Cody??”, although this isn't the focus.

REUBEN

Where’s James?

No one is sure if he is in or not.

There is a knock on the door. “Ah there he is”. Nat opens the door.

POSTMAN

(Fed up with their job)

Hi, I have a parcel for your neighbour.

NAT

(Genuine joy and hope)

That’s great to hear! Good luck.

Without taking the parcel, NAT shuts the door.

CODY

(A little flustered by the absurdness that surrounds him)

So does anyone know if James is here?

BRET

(To upstairs)

YOO JAMES?

JAMES

(Muffled from upstairs)

YEAH?

BRET

DOCUMENTARY TIME BRO.

JAMES

YOOOO.

Offscreen, upstairs, we hear some footsteps, a tumble, and

JAMES

OWWWW.

BRET

YOU OKAY BRO?

ALEX

I’m sure he’s fine.

JAMES

NOOOO.

END OF ACT ONE


A CODY voice-over segues to ACT 2 featuring a house tour and more. Lar-dee-dar.
