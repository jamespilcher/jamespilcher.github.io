Sentiment Analysis on News Headlines Over Time

04.05.2023, 323 words, 1 min 15 secs

In our JunctionX hackathon, one of the tracks was mental health. One of the teams used sentiment analysis to guage how positive/negative a block of text was. In production of this site, I was thinking of cool JS projects to add to the fun section, and this popped back into my head. I wanted to do it on news headlines as a 'how positive is the news!'. Slight problem is that this site is entirely static (courtesy of github pages), meaning all the processing would have to be done on the front end, or through an API. .
